Vanity Fair Antiques and Collectibles Mall began decades ago as a family-owned business. Located on Antiques Row (Fort Street) in the beautiful Victoria, BC, it now houses antiques from dozens of vendors in one convenient place. Come browse our displays and get lost in the shelves filled with vintage treasures. Our friendly and knowledgeable staff help you find what you seek and point out hidden gems. Our collections include jewellery, china, toys, dolls, and even clothing and accessories. No matter what you're looking for, you're sure to find a little piece of history or something you can't live without.
